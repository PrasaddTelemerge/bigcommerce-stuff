Here at DankStop, we get a lot of questions on the different types of nails. To make your life simpler, we’ve created the following infographic below that explains everything you need to know about domed nails, domeless nails, banger nails, universal nails, and many more.

Nails are absolutely essential to the dabbing process. If you’re new to concentrates or want to learn more, check out our article on dab rigs and dabbing. Without a nail, dabbing would be impossible, as it is where you place your legal concentrates and essential oils. DankStop carries a wide variety of nails, and we know that not everybody is an expert on the benefits and drawbacks of each type. By explaining each type of nail and how they function, we hope to reach our ultimate goal of finding you the perfect nail that fits your preferences and your particular vapor rig.

DOMED VS DOMELESS

Domed Nails

These types of nails should be used with a dome to get maximum efficiency. Due to their specific design and how these nails function, having a dome will stop vapor from escaping into the air before it can be inhaled into your concentrate pipe.

  • Glass Nails

    Glass nails are the default nail that comes with most dab rigs. Although they get the job done, glass nails are the most delicate of the 4 materials that are used in crafting the nails that we sell.

    • Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm
    • Joint Genders: Fits Male Only
    • Materials: Glass

  • Adjustable Nails

    Adjustable nails get their name because they give you the option to adjust their height. The purpose of this feature is that these nails can be used with any size dome. With a large dome you can raise the nail while with a smaller dome you can lower the nail, allowing this nail to be at the optimal height no matter what the size of the dome it is paired with.

    • Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm
    • Joint Genders: Fits Male Only
    • Materials: Titanium or Ceramic

Domeless Nails

Domeless nails make the whole process of using a concentrate pipe much simpler. Instead of using two separate tools in a dome and a nail, domeless nails combine the two and are manufactured with specific features that make domes excessive and unnecessary. Domeless nails are also safer because you don’t have to maneuver around the extremely hot nail (with a dome you would have to cap or remove it right near the nail).

Universal → Universal nails refer to any nail that fits one of two categories. It either fits both genders (male and female) or it fits multiple joint sizes. Universal nails are fantastic for their versatility as well as great for someone who owns multiple pieces with differing joint sizes/genders.

Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm

Joint Genders: Available for Male and Female

Materials: Titanium and Ceramic

Completely Universal → Completely universal nails fit not just multiple joint sizes, but also fit both gender joints. They are created in a way that no matter what type of rig you have, these nails will fit and work flawlessly. These nails live up to their name.

Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm

Joint Genders: Fits Both Male and Female

Materials: Titanium and Ceramic

Honey Bucket → Honey Bucket nails are a combination of a banger nail and an inverted dome. The banger sits in an upside-down dome and is attached by a swinging arm. The swinging arm allows you to heat the nail outside of the bucket and place it safely back inside when you are ready to load your concentrates. This type of nail has a slit so that the vapor can travel into the rig.

Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm

Joint Genders: Available for Female and Male

Materials: Quartz

Banger → Banger nails are unique in that they have a curved shape that puts the nail at a distance from your joint. Since you are heating the nail further away from the joint, banger nails serve the function of preventing heat stress or fractures.

Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm

Joint Genders: Available for Female and Male

Materials: Titanium, Quartz, and Ceramic

Direct Inject → Direct inject nails fit directly into a male joint, similar to either a glass nail or an adjustable nail. The difference is that direct inject nails have a dish with a raised bowl, which makes them domeless as a dome is not needed. The vapor travels directly from the dish and is injected into the dab rig straight from the dish where the concentrates are vaporized.

Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm

Joint Genders: Fits Male Joints

Materials: Quartz and Ceramic

Daisy → Daisy nails contain a bowl design with multiple slits lining the circumference of the top of the dish. Concentrates are vaporized and the vapor travels through these slits and into the concentrate pipe.

Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm

Joint Genders: Available for Male and Female

Materials: Quartz

Halo → Halo nails have a very unique appearance that is matched by the functionality of this innovative design. They have a dish for your concentrates with an arch going above it. The arch has numerous slits that allow the vapor to flow upwards into the slits and enter your dab rig.

Available Sizes: 14mm and 18mm

Joint Genders: Available for Both Male and Female

Materials: Quartz

Angled Cut → These nails get their name from the shape of their design. Instead of being flat, these nails are manufactured to have the quartz on a raised angle around the dish. The angle cut design provides an aesthetic appeal and a variation from the standard dish design.

Available Sizes: 10mm, 14mm, and 18mm

Joint Genders: Fits Male and Female

Materials: Quartz

“So...Which one should I get?”

As vague as it seems, the honest answer is “It depends.”. Each type of nail is catered to the specific wants or preferences of different customers. The type of nail you should purchase depends on your dab rig, what material you prefer, and what functionality you are looking for. Our customer support team is extremely informed when it comes to all of our available nails, so don’t hesitate to reach out as we would be glad to walk you through the nail buying process.
